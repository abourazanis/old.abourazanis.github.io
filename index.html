How I started my own Jekyll blog

The idea to start a personal blog was in my mind for quite some time but I could not motivated enough myself to do it. Even after I read various posts suggesting that every developer should have one, as a self note keeper, as a way to improve his communication skills and his 'brand'.

I was feeling that it was not enough fun. Just a WordPress install, a nice looking theme and that's all it would take to build it. Boring.

Then I read about Jekyll, Octopress and all the other static site generators. I was intrigued.
